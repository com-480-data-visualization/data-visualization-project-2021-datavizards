About the constellation

Greetings fellow movie enthusiast!

Have you ever wondered what does it take to make a successful movie? Is it because the director's vision, the actor's performance or perhaps both? How does the actor-director's relationship influence the opinions of millions of viewers? You can answer these questions yourself through our interactive visualization! Before moving on to discover our constellation, please make sure to read the instructions for the best experience.

INSTRUCTIONS: The constellation features director and actor nodes in distinct colors. You can hover over the nodes and links for more details. We encourage you to hold and drag the nodes around for more clarity and fun! The constellation features beautiful transitions as well as details. You can click on a specific director-actor link for a detailed analysis. If you feel lost at any point, make sure to use the search bar at the top left, or simply zoom-in and out, and move around the space freely. You can use the space outside the blue box to move away from the constellation.

Did you know... 'The Godfather' by Francis Coppola, one of our all-time favourites, managed to earn huge profits with a surprisingly small budget! Feel free to use the constellation to discover more such trends in the movie industry. Have fun!
